Evaluate

An evaluation will assess your current social media use and goals, and establish whether or not they’re working. Having a clear plan for how your business will use social media will ensure that the right channels are beings used, this increases your chances for social media success.

Execute

Upon evaluating the best possible methods of how your business can achieve social media success, the next step is will be to execute these methods. This includes creating content, engaging with clients and providing monthly reports along with an editorial calendar.

Elevate

An evaluation inclides measuring the results of your return on investment, this will determine the extent of the campaign's success. Upon measuring these results the strategy will be adjusted accordingly thus contributing to more growth for your business.

Social Media Management

What is Social Media Management? Social Media Management is the management, monitoring, contributing to and guiding the social media presence of a brand, product, individual or corporation.

Why is Social Media Management Important? There are so many opportunities to learn more about your customers and be able to engage in meaningful conversations about your business. Learning about your customers has never been easier with the use of tools such as Facebook and Instagram Insights, obtaining the demographics of your online audience allows for businesses be able to better understand, connect with and cater to the needs of their audience. Social Media is the only Area of Business where you Don't Need to outspend your Competitors in order to beat them. - Unknown

Social Media Marketing

With 3.196 billion people using social media and 11 new people starting to use social media every second, This means that there are about 1 million people every day who are moving forward with technology and being exposed to new brands and businesses. Social Media Marketing is important because it allows for an increase in brand recognition, it is effective and inexpensive and it generates more leads/sales. There is very little risk when getting involved in social media, in fact when done properly there's nothing to lose. Well what are you waiting for?
